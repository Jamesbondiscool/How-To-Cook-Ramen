Ramen Cooking Instructions

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of ramen noodles
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 green onion
  • 1/2 cup of shredded chicken (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot.
  2. Add the ramen noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes until the noodles are tender.
  3. Drain the water and set the noodles aside.
  4. Boil another 2 cups of water in the same pot.
  5. Add the egg and cook for 6-7 minutes until it's hard-boiled.
  6. Remove the egg from the pot and let it cool down. Peel and slice the egg into halves.
  7. Chop the green onion.
  8. If using shredded chicken, add it to the pot and cook for 1-2 minutes until heated through.
  9. Assemble the ramen bowl by adding the cooked noodles, chicken (if using), sliced egg, and chopped green onion.
  10. Pour the hot water from the pot into the bowl.
  11. Serve and enjoy

Note: You can add any other ingredients of your choice such as vegetables, tofu, or shrimp to customize your ramen bowl.

Caution: The water and pot will be hot. Handle with care and use oven mitts if necessary.
